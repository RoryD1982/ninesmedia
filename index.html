META brand launch campaign — "Built for the Untamed." Single-image and video ads targeting adventure, hiking and overlanding communities across SA. Paired with micro-influencer UGC for social proof.

Nomad Gear Co. was launching into a crowded adventure gear market with zero brand recognition. Creative Concept

Google Search ads + dedicated landing page — "Feel Better. Faster." Keyword targeting across wellness, anxiety, fatigue and chronic pain searches in Gauteng, linked to a conversion-optimised booking page.

The Problem

GreenLeaf Clinics had two locations in Gauteng, a good reputation among existing patients, and a Google Ads account that had been running for 18 months — with nothing to show for it. Broad keywords, no conversion tracking, and a homepage used as a landing page meant they were paying for clicks that went nowhere. New patient bookings had stagnated for a year.

The Solution

We rebuilt the Google Ads account from scratch: tight, intent-based keyword targeting, negative keyword lists, and three new campaign-specific landing pages built around a single CTA — Book Now, It's Free. Conversion tracking was installed properly for the first time, giving us real data to optimise against. Within 6 weeks, cost per lead dropped by 40% and bookings hit a new monthly record.
